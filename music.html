The Music of Aaron Torres

My pursuit of excellence in classical guitar has guided me towards playing music by many composers. Some favorites might be the German composer Johann Sebastian Bach, the Spanish music of the great Isaac Albéniz all way to the great works of The Beatles. I enjoy finding music from around the world to play and add my own ideas too. As well, I appreciate making arrangements of music that catches my ear. On my website, you can hear some of the music that I have cherished throughout my life.

Mi meta de la guitarra es conocer música antiguo y nuevo de muchos compositores. Algunos favoritos sean el compositor Alemán Johann Sebastian Bach, la música Español de la gran Isaac Albéniz al las obras de los Beatles. Yo disfruto encontrando música de todo el mundo para jugar y añadir mis propios ideas. Ademas, hago arreglos de la música del jazz y otros tipos. Por mi sitio red, puedes oir la música que he apreciado todo mi vida.

The goal of a musician is to share. In my concerts, I like to share what I have learned and how music continues to inspire me. La meta de un músico es compartir. Por mis conciertos, me gustaría compartir lo que he aprendido y como la música me sigue inspirar.
